Cartograft Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.06.2025

Information We Collect

Cartograft is designed to protect your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • World maps and screenshots you upload
  • Markers and locations you create
  • App settings and preferences

How We Use Information

All data remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your Minecraft world maps
  • Save your markers and custom locations
  • Remember your app preferences

Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from children under 13. All data processing occurs locally on the device.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using standard mobile app storage practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@cartograft.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
